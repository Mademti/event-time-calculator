Event Time Calculator

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-13

Event Time Calculator is a Chrome extension designed to calculate event durations and total hours inside Google Calendar.

Data Access

With your permission, the extension may access Google Calendar event data (such as event titles and start/end times) solely to perform time calculations.

Data Usage

  • Calculate event durations
  • Calculate total hours over selected events

Data Storage & Sharing

Event Time Calculator does not store your Google Calendar data on external servers. Your data is not sold or shared with third parties.

Your Choices

You can remove the extension at any time or revoke access via your Google Account settings.
