Effortlessly Track time spent on Google Calendar events

TrakkyTime lets you calculate and track the total duration of recurring events in Google Calendar, accross any time range. Everything runs locally. Read-only access.

What TrakkyTime does (and does not do)

What it does
  • Reads your Google Calendar events to calculate total time spent over a selected period (day, week, month, year, or custom range).
  • Uses event titles and start/end date-times to compute totals and a simple breakdown.
  • Runs calculations locally in your browser for your own use.
What it does not do
  • Does not create, edit, delete, or share any calendar events.
  • Does not sell, transfer, or store your calendar data on external servers.
  • Does not access data beyond what is needed for time calculations.

Permissions: TrakkyTime requests Google Calendar read-only access (calendar.readonly) to retrieve existing events and calculate totals.
