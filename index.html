Effortlessly track time spent on Google Calendar events

TrakkyTime lets you calculate and track the total duration of recurring events in Google Calendar, across any time range. Everything runs locally. Read-only access.

How TrakkyTime works

TrakkyTime is a lightweight utility designed to help you understand how your time is distributed across activities recorded in Google Calendar. It focuses exclusively on time calculation and transparency, without altering your data.

What the extension does
  • Reads existing Google Calendar events to calculate the total duration of matching events over a selected period (day, week, month, year, or custom date range).
  • Uses only event titles and start/end date-times to compute aggregated totals and a simple per-period breakdown.
  • Processes all calculations locally in your browser for your personal use.
What the extension does not do
  • Does not create, edit, delete, or share any Google Calendar events.
  • Does not store, sell, or transmit your calendar data to external servers.
  • Does not access information beyond what is strictly required for time calculations.

Permissions & privacy: TrakkyTime requests Google Calendar read-only access (calendar.readonly) solely to retrieve existing events and compute time totals. No calendar content is modified, and no personal data is retained outside your browser.
