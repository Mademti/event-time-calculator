Event Time Calculator

Event Time Calculator is a simple Chrome extension that helps you calculate event durations and total hours directly inside Google Calendar.

  • Calculate event duration (start to end)
  • Total hours across multiple events
  • Works directly in Google Calendar

Data & Permissions

The extension accesses your Google Calendar events only to calculate durations and totals you explicitly request.

No data is sold or shared with third parties.
